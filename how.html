How Did I Fall for You?

Jab maine pehli baar aapko dekha na, toh ek studious student jo focused tha, chess player tha… jaane anjaane mein maine aapke saath bhi kaafi matches khele, aur har baar haari, kyunki main ek champion ke saath khel rahi thi. Jaan, bas ek reminder—please firse chess khelna start karo, apni ranking increase karo, I want to see you on the top. Back to the topic—How I fell for you? Ek studious student, jo focused tha, ache marks laata tha, aur sabse badi baat—aap ek bahut acche listener the. Dheere-dheere hum dost bane, phir lockdown lag gaya, hum hostel vacate karke ghar aa gaye. Na jaane kaise, kab hamari itni baatein hone lagi ki poora din nikal jata. Ultimately, I fell for you. Aapki baaton ki aadat si hone lagi… aur phir maine aapko propose kar diya. Haan, uske baad bohot cheezein hui—ups and downs aaye humare relationship mein, lekin humne apne rishte ko aur strong banaya. Aur ab hum 3 saal complete karne wale hain. I hope and wish ki hum poori zindagi saath rahein… aur iss duniya mein aapka last day, mera bhi last day ho. ❤️
